Welcome to Hotel De Luna

Welcome to Hotel De Luna, where the living and departed converge in a realm of enchantment and secrets. Immerse yourself in an extraordinary experience that transcends the boundaries of the mortal world.

Celebrating Father's Day

Treat your dad to an unforgettable experience at Hotel De Luna. Book now for a special tribute to fatherhood and create cherished memories together. Happy Father's Month!

Father's Day Retreat

Luxurious rooms and suites for a comfortable and indulgent stay. Rejuvenating spa treatments to help dad relax and unwind. Indoor and outdoor swimming pools for a refreshing dip. Indoor climbing wall to challenge dad's adventurous spirit. Well-equipped gym to maintain fitness routines. Renowned restaurant offering delectable cuisine for a memorable dining experience.

Thrills and Chills Package

Shooting range for dads to test their marksmanship skills. Exhilarating zip lining activities for an adrenaline rush. Premium accommodations for a comfortable stay. Rooftop swimming pool with stunning views. Access to other hotel amenities such as spa, gym, and restaurant. Unforgettable experiences filled with excitement and adventure.

Family Fun Getaway

Dedicated kids' playroom with games and activities for children's enjoyment. Indoor and outdoor swimming pools for family water fun. Shops inside the hotel for convenient shopping. Theater showcasing live performances for entertainment. Comfortable rooms and suites to accommodate the whole family. Restaurant offering a diverse menu to cater to everyone's taste buds.
